The Hidden Roots of Exam Stress

Understanding the "why" can help you tackle the "how." **Student Stress** often stems from a combination of external pressure and internal fears:

  • Fear of Failure (FOMO-Failing): Deep worry about disappointing family, teachers, or yourself. This is often the biggest driver of high-stakes **Test Anxiety**.
  • The Procrastination Trap: Leaving crucial **Exam Preparation** until the last minute (cramming) ensures your stress levels remain high.
  • The Comparison Culture: Constantly measuring your progress against others sets unrealistic expectations.
  • Lack of Preparation: If you don't start early, the feeling of being overwhelmed becomes a self-fulfilling prophecy.
